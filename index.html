About Bloodworth Music Ministries

We exist to provide students with the opportunity to learn music in a Christ-centered environment, regardless of their financial situation. We believe that every student with a desire to learn should be given the chance to grow, develop their gifts, and use them for God’s glory.

Our goal is not only to teach music, but to invest in each student’s life — building confidence, discipline, and a heart for worship. Through patient instruction and genuine care, we strive to create an environment where students feel encouraged, supported, and inspired.


We are committed to raising up a generation that will use their gifts to glorify God and serve others through music.

What it means to be a donor

Your generosity gives a student the opportunity they may never have had, the chance to learn music, grow in confidence, and use their gifts to glorify God.

Many of our students have the desire, the passion, and the willingness to learn… but not always the resources. Because of you, they are placed in front of caring, committed instructors who don’t just teach music, they invest in lives.

Every donation helps provide instruments, lessons, and a Christ-centered environment where students are encouraged, supported, and reminded that their gifts have purpose.

This is more than a lesson.
This is discipleship through music.

When you give, you’re not just supporting a program, you’re helping shape a life that can worship, serve, and impact others for God’s glory.
